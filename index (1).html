How To Make
Indomie

When you eat something that cooked by
yourself, the happiness is priceless.

Decide a Menu

Ingredients:

  • 1.Water
  • 2.Instant Noodles
  • 3.Stove
  • 4.Pot
  • 5.Plate
  • 6.Flatware

    • Directions

  • first, fill water into the pot.
  • second, put the pot on the stove and turn on the stove.
  • third, wait for the water to boil.
  • after then, when it boils, pour the instant noodles into the pot.
  • then, while waiting for the noodles to cook, prepare a plate.
  • pour instant noodle seasoning on the plate.
  • if the noodles are cooked drain the water, and pour the noodles onto the plate.
  • then stir the noodles and seasonings until mixed.
  • and done! indomie is ready to serve.
